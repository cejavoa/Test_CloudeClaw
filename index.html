How to Use

For best results, follow these simple steps:

  1. Cleanse: Wash your hands with warm water and pat dry thoroughly
  2. Apply: Take a small amount (about the size of a pea) and warm it between your palms
  3. Massage: Gently massage the balm into your hands, focusing on dry areas and cuticles
  4. Absorb: Allow 2-3 minutes for the balm to absorb before touching other surfaces
  5. Repeat: Use 2-3 times daily, or as needed for optimal hydration
  6. Overnight Treatment: Apply generously before bed and wear cotton gloves for intensive care
